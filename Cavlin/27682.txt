<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>18.</strong><em>All the elders were present. </em>We may gather that out of this place which we had already in the fifteenth chapter. So often as any weighty business was to be handled, the elders were wont to come together, to the end the consultation might be more quiet without the multitude.
