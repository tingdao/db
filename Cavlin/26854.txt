<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>29.</strong><em>And, having filled a sponge with vinegar, they fixed it on hyssop. </em>When he says that they <em>fixed the sponge on hyssop, </em>the meaning is, that they fastened it to the end of a bunch of <em>hyssop, </em>that it might be raised to Christ’s mouth; for, in that country, <em>hyssops </em>grow as large as small shrubs, <span class="cite-ref">(180)</span></p><div class="notes"><p><span class="cite-note">(180)</span> “<span class="lang fr-fr">Car l’a les hyssopes sont grans comme petits arbnsseaux</span>.” </p></div></div>
