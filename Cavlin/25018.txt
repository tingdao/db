<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>44.</strong><em>And thinking that he was in the company </em>Many passages of Scripture show plainly, that those who came from a distance, at the festivals, to worship in the temple, were accustomed to travel in <em>companies. </em>There is no reason, therefore, to wonder that, on the first day, Joseph and Mary were less anxious about the child; and their subsequent conduct shows that this was not owing to indolence or carelessness. </p></div>
