<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>48.</strong><em>And immediately one ran. </em>As Christ had once refused to drink, it may be conjectured with probability, that it was repeatedly offered to him for the sake of annoyance; though it is also not improbable that <em>the vinegar </em>was held out to him in a cup before he was raised aloft, and that <em>a sponge </em>was afterwards applied to his mouth, while he was hanging on the cross. </p></div>
