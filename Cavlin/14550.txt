<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>7.</strong><em>All they that hate me whisper together against me. </em>Here he seems generally to include both classes of his enemies; those who sought to oppress him in an open manner, and in the character of avowed enemies; and those who, under the pretense of friendship, attempted to do the same thing by deceit and stratagem. Accordingly, he says that all of them took counsel together about his destruction, just as we know that wicked men hold much secret consultation respecting their intended deeds of treachery, and whisper to one another concerning them. Hence he adds the words <em>to meditate, </em>or <em>plot, </em>which he employs to denote their base conspiracies and sinful consultations. </p></div>
