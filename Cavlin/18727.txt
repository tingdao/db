<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>3.</strong><em>Because thou shalt be multiplied. </em>Now follows the reason why he commanded the cords to be lengthened for enlarging the tents. It is, that a moderate space would not contain a numerous people, whom the Lord will gather into one from every quarter. Now, because Judea was hideous on account of its ruins and desolation, he says that the forsaken cities shall be inhabited. </p></div>
