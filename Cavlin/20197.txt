<div class="comment-content"><p>He confirms the same thing, and shows that the destruction of Babylon would be such, that everything valuable would be destroyed. <em>Fall, </em>he says, <em>shall her strong men in the streets; </em>which is worse than if he had said, “They shall fall in battle.” Babylon was so taken that all her armed men were slain in the middle of the city. Cyrus indeed spared, as it has been already said, the common people; but he slew all the chief men and the armed soldiers.
