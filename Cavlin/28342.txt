Nor is there any doubt but that he commemorates these praises, in order to recommend those whom he praised to the Romans. <span class="cite-ref">(475)</span></p><div class="notes"><p><span class="cite-note">(475)</span> It is said of Mary, that she “labored much<span class="lang el-gr">,” εἰς ἡμᾶς, </span> towards us, or among us; “inter nos — among us,” [<span class="bvs">Beza </span>]; “pro nobis — for us,” [<span class="bvs">Grotius </span>]. The reading<span class="lang el-gr">εἰς ὑμᾶς, </span> towards you, has many MSS. in its favor, and also<span class="lang el-gr">ἐν ὑμῖν, </span> among you. — <em>Ed. </em></p></div></div>
