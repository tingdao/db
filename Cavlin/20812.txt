<div class="comment-content"><p>Here God begins to show the reason why he extenuated the wickedness of Sodom in comparison with that of his own people: for if he had spoken generally, without explaining the counsel of God, his language would have been incredible, and so would have been ineffectual. But now God shows that he did not pronounce rashly what we heard before, namely, that the Jews were worse than the Sodomites. How so? <em>for this was the iniquity of Sodom thy sister, </em>says he, first <em>pride, then fullness of bread</em>, and luxury in which they were in the habit of indulging, and of drowning themselves in ease to enjoy a long peace; afterwards, <em>they did not seize the hand of the poor</em>.
