<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>34.</strong><em>Delivered him to the tormentors, till he should pay all that he owed. </em>The Papists are very ridiculous in endeavoring to light the fire of purgatory by the word <em>till</em>; for it is certain that Christ here points out not temporal death, by which the judgment of God may be satisfied, but eternal death. </p></div>
