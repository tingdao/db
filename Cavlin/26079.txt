<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>34.</strong><em>I saw and testified. </em>He means that what he declares is not doubtful; because God was pleased to make him fully and thoroughly acquainted with those things of which he was to be the witness to the world; and it is worthy of notice, that he <em>testified </em>that Christ was<em>the Son of God, </em>because he who gives the Holy Spirit must be the Christ, for to no other belongs the honor and the office of reconciling men to God. </p></div>
