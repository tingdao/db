<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>32.</strong><em>Causeth her to commit adultery. </em>As the <em>bill of divorcement </em>bore, that the woman had been loosed from her former husband, and might enter into a new marriage, the man who, unjustly and unlawfully, abandons the wife whom God had given him, is justly condemned for having prostituted his wife to others. </p></div>
