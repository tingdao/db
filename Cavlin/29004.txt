<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>15.</strong><em>Whose end shall be. </em>He adds this for the consolation of the pious. For it is the statement of a courageous man, who despises the foolish judgments of men, and patiently waits for the day of the Lord. In the mean time, he shows a singular boldness of conscience, which does not dread the judgment of God. </p></div>
