<div class="comment-content"><p>By <em>perils from the nation </em>he means those that befell him from his own nation, in consequence of the hatred, that was kindled against him among all the Jews. On the other hand, he had the Gentiles as his adversaries; and in the <em>third </em>place snares were laid for him <em>by false brethren. </em>Thus it happened, that </p><p>for Christ’s name’s sake he was hated by all. <LineBreak />(<span class="bvs">Mat_10:22</span>.) </p></div>
