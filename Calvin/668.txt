<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>9.</strong><em>And his sons Isaac and Ishmael buried him</em>. Hence it appears, that although Ishmael had long ago been dismissed, he was not utterly alienated from his father, because he performed the office of a son in celebrating the obsequies of his deceased parent. Ishmael, rather than the other sons did this, as being nearer. </p></div>
