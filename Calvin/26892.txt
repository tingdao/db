<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>25.</strong><em>Unless I see in his hands the print of the nails. </em>This points out the source of the vice to be, that every one wishes to be wise from his own understanding, and flatters himself beyond measure. <em>If I do not see, </em>says he, “and if I do not touch, <em>I will not believe. </em>” <span class="cite-ref">(214)</span> These words have no approach to faith, but it is what may be called a sensual judgment, by which I mean, a judgment which is founded on the perception of the senses, <span class="cite-ref">(215)</span> The same thing happens to all who are so devoted to themselves <span class="cite-ref">(216)</span> that they leave no room for the word of God. It is of no consequence, whether you read <em>the place, </em>or <em>the shape, </em>or The Print <em>of the nails; </em>for transcribers may have exchanged <span class="lang el-gr">τύπον</span> (<em>print</em>) for <span class="lang el-gr">τόπον</span>, (<em>place,</em>) or <span class="lang el-gr">τόπον</span> (<em>place</em>)for <span class="lang el-gr">τύπον</span>, (<em>print;</em>) but the meaning is not altered on that account. Let the reader, therefore, choose which of them he shall prefer. <span class="cite-ref">(217)</span></p><div class="notes"><p><span class="cite-note">(214)</span> “<em class="lang fr-fr">Si je ne voy point, </em><span class="lang fr-fr">dit il, et si je ne touche, je ne croirai point</span>.” </p><p><span class="cite-note">(215)</span> “<span class="lang fr-fr">C’est a dire, qui est fonde sur l’apprehension des sens</span>.” </p><p><span class="cite-note">(216)</span><em>“ </em><span class="lang fr-fr">Qui sont tellement adonnez a leur propre sens</span>.” </p><p><span class="cite-note">(217)</span><em>“ </em><span class="lang fr-fr">Car les deux mots Grecs ne sont point differens qu’en une lettre, et il est aisc de prendre Pun pour l’autre</span>;<em>” </em>— <em>“ </em>for the two Greek words differ only in a single letter, and one of them might easily be taken for the other.” </p></div></div>
