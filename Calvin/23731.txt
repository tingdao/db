<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>3.</strong><em>Unless you are converted. </em>To the example of <em>little children </em>must be referred the <em>conversion </em>of which he now speaks. Hitherto they had been too much habituated to the ordinary customs of men; and if they would gain their object, they must pursue a totally different course. <span class="cite-ref">(499)</span> Every one wished for himself the first or the second rank; but Christ does not allot even the lowest place to any man who does not lose sight of distinctions and <em>humble himself </em>On the contrary, he says, </p><div class="notes"><p><span class="cite-note">(499)</span> “<span class="lang fr-fr">Il leur est besoin de tourner bride, et de s’accoustumer a tout cela</span>;” — “they must wheel round, and get accustomed to all this.” </p></div></div>
