<div class="comment-content"><p><strong>16</strong><em>But avoid profane and unmeaning noises </em>My opinion as to the import of these words has been stated in my commentary on the last chapter of the First Epistle to Timothy; and my readers will find it there. <span class="cite-ref">(176)</span></p><p><em>For they will grow to greater ungodliness. </em>That he may more effectually deter Timothy from that profane and noisy talkativeness, he states that it is a sort of labyrinth, or rather a deep whirlpool, from which they cannot go out, but into which men plunge themselves more and more. </p><div class="notes"><p><span class="cite-note">(176)</span> See p. 173. </p></div></div>
