<div class="comment-content"><p><span class="bvs">Mat_8:9</span><strong>.</strong><em>For I am a man subject to the power of another </em>This comparison does not imply equality between the two cases, but is taken from the less to the greater. He forms a higher conception of the divine power, which is manifested in Christ, than of the authority which was possessed by himself over servants and soldiers. </p></div>
